# Objection Handling Guide — Betwaggle Affiliate Outreach

## Top 10 Objections and Responses

### 1. "We already have a sponsor for our tournament"
**Response:** "That's great — Waggle isn't a sponsorship. It's a digital amenity that runs alongside your existing sponsors. In fact, it gives sponsors MORE visibility because their name appears on the live leaderboard that every player checks all day. We'd never compete with your sponsors — we make them look better."

### 2. "Gambling is against our club policy"
**Response:** "I completely understand, and I want to be clear — Waggle isn't casino gambling. It's the same nassau, skins, and side action your members already play every weekend. We just automate the scorekeeping and settlement so nobody's doing math on the back of a scorecard. Think of it as a really smart scorecard that handles the friendly wagers your players are already making."

### 3. "I don't have time to set this up"
**Response:** "That's the best part — you don't set up anything. Players scan a QR code at registration. We print the code, we handle the tech, we manage the settlement. Your staff doesn't touch a thing. The only 'work' you do is put our QR code next to the pin sheets, and collect your commission check."

### 4. "What's the catch? Why is it free?"
**Response:** "No catch. Our business model is simple: players pay $32 or $149 per event depending on the format. We keep our share, you keep 25%. If nobody uses it, nobody pays anything — including you. We're incentivized to make the product great because we only make money when your players love it."

### 5. "Our members are older / not tech-savvy"
**Response:** "We built Waggle specifically for the golf course, not for tech people. There's no app to download — it works on any phone's browser. The interface is designed for sunlight readability with big buttons. If your members can check a text message, they can use Waggle. And for members who don't want to use it, it doesn't affect their round at all."

### 6. "We tried something like this before and it didn't work"
**Response:** "I'd love to hear what went wrong — honestly, that helps me. Most tools we've heard about required app downloads or were too complicated for on-course use. Waggle is browser-based (no download), pre-loaded with your course data, and designed for the pace of play. Would you be open to a free trial on one event so you can see the difference?"

### 7. "How do I know you'll actually pay the commissions?"
**Response:** "Fair question. You get a dashboard at betwaggle.com/affiliate/dashboard where you can see every referral, every event, and every commission in real time. Payouts happen monthly. We're a real company, not a fly-by-night — our system handles the tracking automatically so there's no room for disputes."

### 8. "I need to run this by my board / ownership"
**Response:** "Absolutely. I can send you a one-pager that explains the program — it's specifically designed for board presentations. The key points: zero cost, zero risk, passive revenue, better player experience. Would that be helpful?"

### 9. "We don't host that many events"
**Response:** "Even one scramble a year is worth it — that's $37 in your pocket for doing nothing. But here's the thing: once your members try Waggle during a scramble, they start using it for their weekend nassaus and skins games on their own. That's where the volume comes from — organic adoption by your regular players."

### 10. "Send me some information and I'll look at it"
**Response:** "Absolutely — I'll send you a link to a live demo right now so you can actually play with it, not just read about it. What's the best email? And if you have a scramble or outing coming up, I can customize the demo with your course data so it looks exactly like what your players would see."
