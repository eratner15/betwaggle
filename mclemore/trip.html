The Highlands
Top 100 Public
Rees Jones & Bill Bergin · Par 71 · 7,055 yds

Golf Digest Top 100 Greatest Public Course. Three hole categories: Canyon Holes (2, 3, 6, 7, 9) play into canyon walls, Highland Holes (4, 5, 8, 10-16) sit on the plateau like an island in the sky, and Cliff Edge Holes (1, 17, 18) overlook historic McLemore Cove.

Hole 18 was named "Best finishing hole in America since 2000" by Golf Digest.
