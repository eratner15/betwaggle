Tomorrow's Games

Three games stacked so every hole has action — main, team match, and side skins.

Main

Team

Best 3 of 4 from Group A vs all 3 from Group B. Group A gets +1 stroke handicap allowance to even up the D1 differential.

Why this stack

Stableford keeps the high-handicap players engaged on every hole. The 4v3 team match creates rooting interest within each group. Skins runs in parallel and lets one good hole pay big.
