Frequently Asked Questions

What happens after my event?
Your event data stays available for 30 days after the event ends. You can view final results, settlement summaries, and share the recap link with your group at any time. After 30 days, the event is archived but can be restored on request.
Can I switch to a season pass later?
Yes. Start with $32/event anytime, then switch to $149/season pass when your group wants recurring events. Your event data stays intact.
Do players need to pay?
No. Only the event organizer pays. Every player in your group gets full access on their phone through a shared link. No accounts, no app download, no per-player fees. Just open the link and play.
How does settlement work?
When your round ends, Waggle calculates exactly who owes what based on all the games and side action. On Buddies Trip and above, each player gets a personalized settlement card with one-tap Venmo deep links. No more spreadsheets, no more arguments at the 19th hole.
