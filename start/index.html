Before you ask
The usual questions.
Do I need to download anything?
No. It is a web app. Works on any phone browser, no app store required. Add to your home screen and it looks like an app.
What if we lose signal on the course?
Scores queue locally and sync the moment you get bars back. Built for Pinehurst Wi-Fi, not the office.
Do handicaps work?
Yes. GHIN lookup on create, strokes applied per hole via the stroke index. Gross and net tracked separately.
Who pays the $32?
Whoever sets up the trip. One charge, the whole group plays. Usually the host. Sometimes the loser at the 19th.
Can I see it before I pay?
Yes. Open the live demo and tap around. No sign-in, no email gate.
