1. Quick Start

Accessing Admin

  1. Open betwaggle.com/app/ on your phone or iPad.
  2. Tap the Admin tab (shield icon) in the bottom nav.
  3. Enter the admin PIN and tap Enter.
  4. You'll see the admin dashboard with tabs: Take Bet, Scores, Book, Lines, Players.

Admin Tabs Overview

TabWhat It Does
Take BetPlace bets for walk-up bettors. Name → Match → Stake → Confirm.
ScoresEnter match results. One-tap scoring grid by flight/round.
BookView all active/settled bets, exposure, and P&L.
LinesMove odds up/down, lock matches from betting.
PlayersRegister players, add credits, view balances.
Tip: Bookmark it. Add the app to your home screen (Share → Add to Home Screen) for full-screen mode and faster access between rounds.

2. Taking Bets

The Take Bet Flow

  1. Select the bettor — type or pick from the player dropdown. Their credit balance shows automatically.
  2. Pick the match — tap the team they want to back. Current odds display next to each team.
  3. Set the stake — use preset buttons ($10, $25, $50) or type a custom amount. Max single bet: $100.
  4. Confirm — review the bet slip (team, odds, stake, to-win) and tap Place Bet.

Walk-Up Bets

When someone walks up to place a bet:

  • If they're not registered, go to Players tab first and create them with starting credits.
  • Come back to Take Bet, select their name, and proceed.
  • Credits are deducted automatically on placement and refunded on void.

Preset Stakes

The $10 / $25 / $50 buttons are designed for speed. For amounts above $50, type it in. The system enforces a $100 max per single bet.

Credit Check: The server validates credits before accepting any bet. If a player tries to bet more than their balance, the bet is rejected with a clear error message. No manual checking needed.

3. Entering Scores

Dense Round View

  1. Go to Scores tab.
  2. Select the flight and round from the dropdowns at top.
  3. Each match shows a scoring grid — tap the final score (e.g., 7-3, 6-4, 5-5, 4-6, 3-7).
  4. The match immediately finalizes and bets auto-settle.

One-Tap Scoring

Each match shows 5 possible outcomes. Tap once to set. Tap the same score again to revert (unfinalizes the match).

Settle Round Button

After entering scores for all matches in a round, tap Settle Round to finalize them all at once. This triggers server-side settlement for all bet types.

Reverting a Settled Match: If you tap a finalized score to revert it and that match has settled bets, you'll get a confirmation dialog. Reverting unsettles those bets. An announcement is logged automatically for the paper trail.

4. Managing the Book

The Book Tab

The Book tab shows your real-time position:

MetricWhat It Means
Total WageredSum of all stakes placed (active + settled).
Active WageredStakes on unsettled bets — money still at risk.
ExposureMax possible payout on active bets minus stakes. This is what you could lose.
Settled P&LHouse profit/loss on completed bets. Positive = house is winning.

Reading the Danger Board

Watch for these warning signs:

  • Exposure > $300 on one match: Consider moving the line or locking the match.
  • One bettor has 5+ active bets: They may be chasing — monitor.
  • All money on one side: You're fully exposed — move the line to attract the other side.

Voiding a Bet

In the Book tab, find the bet and tap Void. This:

  • Cancels the bet immediately.
  • Refunds the bettor's credits automatically.
  • Records the void with a timestamp.
When to Void: Void if a bet was placed in error, on the wrong match, or if both parties agree to cancel. Don't void just because the bettor is losing.

5. Line Management

Why Move Lines?

Lines (odds) should move when:

  • All the money is on one side — move to attract action on the other side.
  • You learn new information (player injury, warmup performance).
  • Exposure on one match is getting too high.

How to Move Lines

  1. Go to Lines tab.
  2. Find the match. Current odds show for both sides.
  3. Tap +25 or -25 next to either team to shift odds in 25-point increments.
  4. Both sides move inversely — making one team a bigger favorite makes the other a bigger underdog.
  5. Tap Reset to return to the algorithm's original line.

What the Percentages Mean

OddsImplied Win %Meaning
-200~67%Strong favorite. Bet $200 to win $100.
-135~57%Moderate favorite (typical with vig).
EVEN50%Coin flip.
+120~45%Moderate underdog (typical with vig).
+200~33%Clear underdog. Bet $100 to win $200.

Locking a Match

Tap the Lock button to freeze betting on a match. Use this when:

  • A match is about to start and you want to cut off betting.
  • Exposure is maxed and you don't want more action.
  • Something seems off (possible sharp action).
The Vig: A 10% house margin (5% juice per side) is built into all odds automatically. A true 60/40 match shows as roughly -135/+120 instead of -150/+150. This is your edge — it's how the book stays profitable over time.

6. Player Management

Registering Players

  1. Go to Players tab.
  2. Tap New Player.
  3. Enter their name and starting credits (default $50).
  4. They can now place bets up to their credit balance.

Adding Credits

Tap the + button next to a player's name. Enter the amount. Credits are added instantly.

Use negative amounts to deduct credits if needed (e.g., cash settlement mid-tournament).

Checking Balances

The Players tab shows every registered player with their current credit balance and total wagered. Players also see their own balance in the betting screen.

Handling Disputes

  • "I didn't place that bet" — check the Book tab for the bet timestamp and bettor name. Void if confirmed error.
  • "My credits are wrong" — review their bet history. Each bet deducts credits; voids refund them. Add credits to correct.
  • "The odds changed on me" — bets lock in the odds at time of placement. Subsequent line moves don't affect existing bets.

7. Settlement

How Auto-Settlement Works

When a match is finalized (score entered and status set to "final"), the system automatically settles all active bets on that match:

Bet TypeSettlement Logic
Match WinnerWinning team → bet wins (payout = stake × decimal odds). Losing team → bet loses. Draw → team bets push (stake returned), draw bets win.
Match MarginExact score match (e.g., "7-3") → bet wins. Any other score → bet loses.
Flight WinnerSettles when ALL matches in the flight are final. Team with most points wins. Tiebreaker: head-to-head result.

Push Rules

A push returns the full stake to the bettor. Pushes happen when:

  • A match ends in a draw (5-5) and the bet was on a team (not on "draw").

Settlement Happens Twice

Settlement runs both client-side (instant visual update) and server-side (authoritative). The server is the source of truth. If there's ever a discrepancy, the server version wins on the next sync (every 30 seconds).

Tip: After entering all scores for a round, tap Settle Round to batch-finalize. This is faster than tapping each match individually and ensures server-side settlement fires for all bet types.

8. Troubleshooting

Stale Data / Not Updating

  • Tap the Sync button (or pull down to refresh).
  • The app syncs every 30 seconds automatically. If data looks stale, force a sync.
  • Clear browser cache if the app seems stuck on an old version.

Bet Rejected

ErrorCauseFix
"Insufficient credits"Player doesn't have enough balance.Add credits in Players tab or reduce stake.
"Max single bet is $100"Stake exceeds per-bet limit.Split into two smaller bets.
"Max exposure per match"Total wagered on this match hit $500 cap.Lock the match or wait for settlement.
"Max 10 active bets"Bettor has 10 unsettled bets.Wait for matches to settle or void stale bets.
"Player not found"Bettor isn't registered.Register them in Players tab first.

Offline / No Signal

The app works offline for viewing. Bets placed offline are queued and auto-submitted when connectivity returns. A "pending" badge appears on queued bets.

Disputed Bet

  1. Find the bet in the Book tab (filter by bettor name).
  2. Check the timestamp, odds, and stake.
  3. If the bet was placed in error, void it (credits auto-refund).
  4. If it's a legitimate dispute about odds, the locked-in odds at placement time are final.

9. End of Tournament

Final Settlement Checklist

  1. Verify all scores are entered — check each flight, each round. No match should show "scheduled".
  2. Settle all rounds — use Settle Round for each round if not already done.
  3. Check flight winner bets — these only settle when ALL matches in a flight are final.
  4. Review the Book — look for any "active" bets that should have settled. Investigate and resolve.
  5. Export data — tap Export Data in Admin. This downloads a JSON file with all matches, bets, players, and settings.
  6. Settle IOUs — use the player credit balances to determine who owes what.

Settling IOUs

After all bets are settled:

  • Credits > starting amount: Player is owed money (winnings).
  • Credits < starting amount: Player owes the book (losses).
  • Credits = starting amount: Player broke even.

The P&L section in the Book tab shows the house position. Positive P&L = the book collected more than it paid out.

Exporting Data

The export includes: all matches with scores, all bets with settlement status, all player balances, odds overrides, and announcements. Save this file for your records.

10. Rules of the Book

Bet Limits

LimitAmount
Max single bet$100
Max exposure per match$500 total wagered
Max active bets per bettor10
Min bet$1

Odds & Vig

  • All odds include a 10% house margin (5% juice per side).
  • Odds are generated from a handicap-based probability model with bilinear interpolation.
  • The GM can move lines manually in 25-point increments.
  • Odds are locked at the moment of bet placement — later line moves don't affect existing bets.

Void Policy

  • Bets can only be voided by the GM (admin).
  • Voiding refunds the full stake to the bettor's credits.
  • Valid reasons to void: duplicate bet, wrong match selected, player error confirmed by both parties.
  • Bets are not voided because a bettor changed their mind or because they're losing.

Dispute Resolution

  1. The GM's decision is final on all bet-related disputes.
  2. The system's recorded odds and timestamps are the source of truth.
  3. If a match result is disputed, the official scorer's card determines the outcome.
  4. In case of a scoring error discovered after settlement, the GM may revert and re-enter the correct score. All affected bets will be re-settled.

Match Cancellation

  • If a match cannot be completed (weather, injury), all bets on that match are voided.
  • Use the Void button on each affected bet in the Book tab.
Golden Rule: When in doubt, the house always errs on the side of the bettor. This is a social game among friends — the goal is fun, not profit maximization. A happy bettor comes back next year.
