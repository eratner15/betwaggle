How It Works

Four steps.
One link to your group.

I.

Set up your event

Pick your games, set handicaps, add players. Five minutes. No account required.

II.

Share one link

Everyone opens the live sportsbook on their phone. Place bets before or during the round.

III.

Score hole by hole

One person enters gross scores. Odds, standings, and pots update on every phone instantly.

IV.

Share the settlement card

Who won. What they're owed. Drop it in the group chat. No arguments.

Game Formats

Eight games. Run simultaneously.

Nassau Front nine, back nine, and total. Live standings update hole by hole. Press button when 2 down.
Skins Pot multiplier tracks carryovers in real time. Win the hole outright to collect.
Wolf Rotating wolf picks a partner (or goes lone wolf) before each hole. Order enforced automatically.
Vegas Team scores form a two-digit number. Difference in numbers = points. Calculated exactly right.
Stableford Points for birdies and eagles, deductions for bogeys. Net leaderboard after every hole.
Banker Rotating banker sets the line. Everyone bets against the banker each hole.
Bloodsome Shamble format — best drive, then alternate shot. Team net score per hole.
Stroke Play Net leaderboard updates after every hole. Handicap strokes applied automatically.
